Gamechngrz: Universe

NE1:Plays Inc.

Lead Programmer

Gamechngrz: Universe is a suite of games intended to test career-related aptitudes in high-school students. These aptitudes are then combined with a student's reported interests to provide a roadmap to the career that best aligns with these parameters. It also acts as a hub for career exploration, allowing students to see live reports of a number of career related data-points, including expected education level, salary, and average career length.

Techability Coding School

Techability Inc.

Unity Curriculum Developer

Techability Inc. is an entirely online school for K-12 students to learn programming. Curriculum at Techability is divided at the 4th grade level, where students below this grade level are taught in MIT's Scratch platform, while the remaining students are taught in Unity. Each curriculum unit spans one month, in which students spend their time in class learning how to use their respective tools and how to apply programming concepts to make games. Students are also asked to follow along with a new tutorial series, posted each month, that walks the student through creation of a complete game project.

EDGE is an open-world RPG where the player character, Thea, must navigate an expansive world of islands in the sky to recruit new inhabitants for her village. Thea must build each recuit their very own home, allowing Thea to control the size and shape of the village. Little Lake

Tiny Boat

Technical Director

Little Lake is an open-world fishing adventure, in which Nulia, daughter of the legendary fisherman Koga, must return to her home town on Lake Little to save her father, after he was found unconscious while trying to track down "The Big One". Nulia must re-kindle the love for fishing her father instilled in her, and use it to retrace her father's steps in hopes of finding a cure for his seemingly un-treatable illness. She must utilize a slew of fishing equipment including a boat, traps, magical skipping stones and more to earn the trust and respect of her father's closest peers in an effort to learn just what her father was trying to do.

Gamechngrz: Universe (Godot Redesign)

NE1:Plays Inc.

Lead Programmer

The Godot redesign of Gamechngrz: Universe takes the core principles of the original Unity game, and abstracts it into a platform, rather than a single game. Content is presented not through gameplay or thematic events but instead a unified menu system, complete with live updated goals and a sortable, modern UI. This redesign was intended to bring the project more in-line with other game and software suites the current generation of students are familiar with.

Involvement Timeline:

October 2023 - February 2024

Job description:

Ported all relevant, existing systems from Unity version of this project to Godot. Re-wrote systems in GDScript, including some full re-writes and some translations. Re-wrote webservice hooks to match with Godot standards. Built modern menu structure, including favoriting games, dynamic games list, and a student profile. Implemented art assets as ported from Unity, and designed UI art to replace placeholders wherever necessary.

Technologies Utilized:

Godot, MySQL, PHP
